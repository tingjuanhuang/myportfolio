SWF｜微笑福利基金會

微笑福利基金會是一個基於慈善的應用程序，它為來自家庭功能失調的孩子捐贈必需品、食物和其他物品，並為有需要的兒童提供緊急救濟。
此外，我們提供課後輔導、學習生活技能和教育知能，使獲得穩定和可持續發展的自立機會，累積內在能量與外在資源，脫離困境，邁向有希望的未來。

Smile Welfare Foundation (SWF) is a charity-based app that donates essential goods, meals, and others for children from dysfunctional families, as well as emergency relief for children in need. In addition, we provide after-school tutoring, learning life skills, and educational program to obtain stable and sustainable opportunities for self-reliance, accumulate internal energy and external resources, and get out of the predicament, and towards a promising future.
